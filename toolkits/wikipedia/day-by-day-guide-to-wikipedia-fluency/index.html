More about Day-by-Day Guide to Wikipedia Fluency

Fluency on the Wikimedia Project websites is imperative, as it allows for better quality contributions and enables you to lead new people to contribute as well. Contributing daily and exploring the different types of editing activity is what Wikipedia fluency is about.

Everyday, for one hour, Wikipedians should try adding content, checking sources, correcting typos, rewriting for better language, fixing vandalism, collaborating with others and so on. After a week of daily contributions you should be fluent in Wikipedia. Begin everyday by reading the main page of community discussions. Familiarise yourself with other community members and learn to share ideas.
